About GovtJobs Daily

Our Mission

GovtJobs Daily was created with a single mission: to help millions of Indian job seekers find government employment opportunities faster and more reliably than any other platform. We believe that access to verified, timely government job information is a fundamental right for every aspirant, and we work tirelessly to deliver that access 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

What We Do

We monitor over 10 official government job sources continuously using automated systems that check for new notifications every single hour. Our sources include Employment News, Sarkari Result, Jagran Josh, and direct feeds from state public service commissions, SSC, UPSC, Railway Recruitment Boards, and banking examination bodies. Each job listing undergoes automated verification to ensure the information is accurate and the official links are functional before being published.

Our Data Sources

We aggregate government job notifications from the following verified official sources:

  • Employment News (employmentnews.gov.in)
  • Sarkari Result
  • Jagran Josh
  • State Public Service Commissions
  • Staff Selection Commission (SSC)
  • Union Public Service Commission (UPSC)
  • Railway Recruitment Boards (RRB)
  • Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS)
  • State-level recruitment portals

Why Trust GovtJobs Daily

Verified Information: Every job listing includes direct links to official government websites. We never redirect you to third-party or misleading pages. Our automated systems verify each link before publishing.

Hourly Updates: Unlike other platforms that update once or twice daily, GovtJobs Daily checks every hour. This means you get the fastest possible alerts when new notifications are released, giving you more time to apply.

Comprehensive Coverage: We cover all types of government employment including central, state, PSU, railway, banking, defence, teaching, police, and all other departments across all 35+ states and union territories.

Free Forever: GovtJobs Daily is and will always be completely free. No registration, no subscription fees, no hidden charges.
